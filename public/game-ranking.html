Best games ever according to Lubos

My top 5 videogames of all-time

Baldur's Gate 3

The best RPG game with deep story, where choices really matter and almost every character reacts to your deeds.

World of Warcraft

An MMORPG game I grew up playing. The game will soon be turning 20 years old, but it's still reigning as the most played game in its genre.

The Elder Scrolls: Skyrim

An epic RPG with unrivalled freedom and massive open world. It's modding community is very active and you can literally make this game your own.

Final Fantasy XIV

Another MMORPG on this list is 10 years younger, although for all you story-oriented, this one has a lot deeper story than WoW, and is very very much viable to be played solo all the way down to the endgame.

The Mass Effect Trilogy

When I want a break from my favourite medieval fantasy setting, I pick up this sci-fi fantasy masterpiece. The fourth installment is a bit lacking, but the original trilogy is very much worth playing through. Even several times.
